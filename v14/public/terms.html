1. The Service

Pallet Storage Near Me ("PSNM", "we", "us") provides ambient pallet storage, goods-in handling, and goods-out handling at Unit 3C Hellaby Industrial Estate, Rotherham S66 8HR. The facility offers approximately 1,602 pallet positions across racked, aisle floor, and open floor storage.

2. Minimum Terms

  • Minimum storage quantity: 5 pallets.
  • Minimum storage period: 4 weeks.
  • After the minimum term, storage continues on a rolling weekly basis.
  • Notice to vacate: 30 days' written notice required after the minimum term has elapsed.

3. Pricing

Current pricing is published at palletstoragenearme.co.uk/quote and is subject to change with 30 days' notice. Pricing tiers are:

  • 1–49 pallets: £3.95 per pallet per week
  • 50–149 pallets: £3.45 per pallet per week
  • 150+ pallets: £2.95 per pallet per week

Handling charges apply per movement: £3.50 per pallet goods-in, £3.50 per pallet goods-out. An onboarding administration fee of £50 applies to new customers storing fewer than 50 pallets; this fee is waived for 50+ pallet agreements.

Invoices are issued weekly or monthly in advance as agreed. Payment is due within 7 days of invoice date. Late payment may result in suspension of access and, after 14 days, removal of goods at the customer's expense.
